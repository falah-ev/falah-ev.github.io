General Information

We are a non-profit organization fucusing on bridging the gap between academic student's academic and professional endeavours. We provide required concise workshops and informal startup environment, where organized student teams, in their free time, work on various social and technical projects. We started since 2016 with 4 core team members and 13 volunteers, collaborating in Charity Week project, which includes organizing social fundraising events for financing education and healthcare for orphan children throughout the world. We have grown since then in our members, volunteers and projects. Thus for a sustained growth, we have come together as a professional-student amalgam, FALAH, which literally means success or self improvement; a community of diverse individuals, students, professionals and locals.
